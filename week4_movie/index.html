ABOUT The Movie Good Will Hunting (1997)

Good Will Hunting is a film directed by Gus Van Sant, and written by Ben Affleck and Matt Damon. The movie takes place in South Boston, following the trials and development of Will Hunting. A janitor at MIT who happens to secretly be a mathematical genius, whose talents are later discovered by a professor, Dr. Sean Maguire. Both Will and Maguire grow to develop a profound relationship, based on their own past trauma and self doubts.


Company credits: Miramax Films, Be Gentlemen, Lawrence Bender Productions

Release Date: December 5th, 1997

Genre: Drama, Romance

Rating: R

Running Time: 126 Minutes
